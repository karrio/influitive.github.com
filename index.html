Engage

Engage your VIP customers with a fun, game-like experience. Present advocate challenges that they find fun and rewarding. Reward them in a tasteful and transparent way.

Nurture

Invite your customers to join your advocacy/reference program. Understand their unique preferences and interests, and deliver them the experience they are seeking.

Connect

Connect your advocates and prospects at every phase of the buying process, not just at the end reference stage. Match them based on their buying needs and their mutual social connections.

Manage all aspects of the advocate relationship – references, referrals and public communications – in one centralized cloud-based platform. Integrated seamlessly with LinkedIn and salesforce.com.

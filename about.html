We believe that mobilizing customer advocates represents the future of marketing. Mobilized and engaged advocates are able to drive targeted awareness, accelerate buying processes and help close deals more effectively than traditional investments in marketing and sales. We see a world where the first instinct of a marketer faced with a tough challenge is to rally their advocates. An advocate-centered world is better place for all of us. In the future, companies will be compelled more than ever to produce fantastic products and services that turn customers into evangelical fans.

Our Team.

Mark Organ

Mark is the founder and CEO of Influitive, a provider of customer advocacy program software for complex sales process companies. Mark enjoys the entrepreneurial process, creating new industries that did not previously exist, and driving immense value for customers, employees and shareholders.

Prior to Influitive, Mark was a co-founder and CEO of Eloqua Corporation, a global leader and innovator in marketing automation. Mark grew the company to profitability, a global presence, and several hundred successful customers and employees. Before founding Eloqua, Mark was a consultant at Bain & Company.

Mark holds a M. S. in Neurosciences from Northwestern University and a B. Sc. in Life Sciences (first class Honours) from Queen’s University.

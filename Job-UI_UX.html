Job Description

User Experience Architect

Are you looking to apply your awesome user experience skills to build a category-defining product, scaling to thousands of businesses and millions of users? Want to join a team that has deep respect for your craft, where you will be an integral and central member of the team?

We are looking for a User Experience Architect with a fresh, vibrant design style and a passion for user success and efficiency. You will apply knowledge of user-centered design methods, human-computer interaction, and industry best practices to drive the most usable interface designs for our groundbreaking advocate management and mobilization product, AdvocateHub, in addition to other products on the drawing board. You strike the right balance between creative and analytical thinking, and participate in concept development, user research, and user acceptance processes.

Qualifications

    Must be able to understand a target audience's needs and other motivating factors and translate them into concepts for compelling interactions and experiences.
    Experience with both B2B SaaS and consumer applications
    Experience with design applications such as Illustrator, Photoshop, Balsamiq, etc. and technologies such as XML, HTML and CSS.
    Strong background in and understanding of the following: user research, information architecture, information design, interaction design, and user interface design.
    Expert knowledge of fundamental digital marketing and search engine optimization best practices as it relates to UX design.

Requirements

    Translate business needs and requirements into engaging and compelling online experiences.
    Work closely with product managers, developers and QA engineers in an agile environment to release quality products efficiently.
    Effective in using wireframes, HTML/JS to communicate your ideas.
    Responsible for creating, enhancing, and articulating the overall brand tone and personality through design and execution of online user experiences.
    Support the development and execution of a communication and education plan for the user experience architecture.

Why do you want this Job?

  • A terrific culture and flat organization. Your voice WILL be heard. You will make an impact, not just on the product, but on the company itself.
  • Work on a visionary product that has a real opportunity to change the world.
  • The best balance of rapid company growth and the security of solid funding, successful business model and experienced management.
  • Generous stock option grant. Our employees are company owners and have a meaningful stake in its successful outcome.
  • Nice 'Cadillac' benefits administered by TriNet.
  • Lots of opportunity to lead, and increase responsibility and compensation.

About Influitive

Influitive is a First Round Capital-backed software company and an emerging leader in Advocate Management and Mobilization technology. As the buying process has become more social in nature, with buyers massively increasing their dependence on knowledgable peers, marketing practitioners require a new kind of platform to mobilize their army of advocates, fans, promoters and evangelists. We call the platform AdvocateHub. AdvocateHub combines the best elements of B2B CRM and consumer social gaming functionality to create an engaging experience for advocates and a straightforward administration experience for our marketer users.

AdvocateHub is in use by some of the most sophisticated and successful business-to-business software service companies, including Xactly, Ektron, Eloqua, ActOn, Brainshark, Dell KACE and ServiceNow.
